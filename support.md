# 5MinsMeditation Support

## Contact

If you need help with 5MinsMeditation, have a bug to report, or want to suggest an improvement, contact:

- Email: support@5minsmeditation.app

## What To Include

To help resolve issues faster, include:

- Your device model
- Your iOS version
- The app version
- A short description of the issue
- Screenshots if relevant

## Common Help

### The timer is not behaving as expected

- Close and reopen the app
- Restart the meditation session
- Make sure your device is running the latest iOS version available to you

### The app does not open correctly

- Force close the app and reopen it
- Restart your device
- Reinstall the app if the issue continues

## Response Time

We aim to respond to support requests within 3 to 5 business days.
