# 5MinsMeditation Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

## Overview

5MinsMeditation is designed to provide a simple meditation timer experience with minimal distraction.

## Data Collection

5MinsMeditation does not intentionally collect, sell, or share personal information.

At this time, the app does not require account creation and does not ask for personal profile details.

## Health Data

5MinsMeditation does not read or write Apple Health data.

## Analytics and Tracking

5MinsMeditation does not use third-party advertising trackers.

If analytics or crash reporting tools are added in a future release, this policy should be updated before release.

## Local Data

The app may store limited app data locally on your device to support the experience. This data is not used to identify you personally.

## Data Sharing

5MinsMeditation does not sell your personal data and does not share personal data with third parties for advertising purposes.

## Children's Privacy

5MinsMeditation is not directed to children under 13, and the app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes

This policy may be updated over time. Any material updates should be reflected in a revised date at the top of this page.
